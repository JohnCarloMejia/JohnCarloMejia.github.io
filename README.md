## Featured Projects

### **Low-Cost FPV Drone for Public Safety Applications**  
*Independent Engineering Passion Project — Summer 2026*

This project explores the design, construction, and testing of a low-cost first-person-view (FPV) quadrotor to evaluate whether small unmanned aerial systems can provide rapid situational awareness in urban environments.

Motivated by recurring illegal fireworks activity in my local community, the project focuses on developing a **mechanically robust, lightweight, and repairable airframe** capable of supporting short-duration observation tasks under strict cost constraints.

**Mechanical engineering focus areas include:**
- Airframe design and structural layout  
- CAD modeling and component packaging  
- Weight optimization and center-of-mass management  
- Manufacturability using 3D printing  
- Structural durability and crash survivability  

Electrical and embedded systems were integrated as required to support the mechanical platform and enable full system testing.
